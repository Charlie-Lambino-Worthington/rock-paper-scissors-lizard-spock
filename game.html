How to play?

The game of Rock Paper Scissors Lizard Spock (often abbreviated as RPSLS) is a variation of the classic game that introduces two new elements: "Lizard" and "Spock". The game is played in rounds, and the objective is to reach 10 points first. Here's how it works:
Scoring System: Players score points based on the outcome of each round. A win earns you 1 point.
Winning Conditions:

  • Rock crushes Scissors
  • Scissors cuts Paper
  • Paper covers Rock
  • Rock crushes Lizard
  • Lizard poisons Spock
  • vaporizes Rock
  • Spock smashes Scissors
  • Scissors decapitate Lizard
  • Lizard eats Paper
  • Paper disproves Spock

Special Conditions:
If both players choose the same gesture, it's a tie, and neither player scores any points for that round. Gameplay:
Players take turns choosing one of the five gestures (Rock, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock). After each round, the player who chose the winning gesture scores 1 point. The game continues until one player reaches 10 points.
Tiebreaker:
In case of a tie at 9-9, the final round becomes crucial. The player who wins this decisive round wins the game outright, regardless of their previous scores. This version adds depth to the traditional game by introducing additional strategies and outcomes, making it more engaging and strategic..
